Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter or oil
  • Baking powder
  • Vanilla extract
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk wet ingredients separately.
  3. Combine wet and dry mixtures.
  4. Pour batter into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool before slicing and serving.
