Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to a high temperature (450–500°F).
  2. Spread tomato sauce over the rolled-out dough.
  3. Add slices of fresh mozzarella.
  4. Bake until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  5. Top with fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil.
